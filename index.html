The Legend of Pepe

Pepe, a courageous Mexican villager, resides in a peaceful little village that finds itself unexpectedly threatened by a flock of wild chickens. Known for his agility and bravery, Pepe bravely accepts the challenge to protect his village. With a calm resolve and a dash of heroism, he faces the feathered foes, aiming to bring back peace and order to the place he calls home.
